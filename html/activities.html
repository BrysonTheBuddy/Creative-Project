Come Join Us!

Our Institute schedule can be found on our Announcements page. Other activities are posted weekly in the newsletter, a month at a glance. We play cards most Monday nights after FHE, and volleyball most Thursday nights at 7pm at the church. All are welcome to come join in the fun!

We also periodically host dance lessons (Salsa, Line dancing, Ballroom Dancing) and do service together. These activities are posted as they come up in the ward bulletin and newsletter.
